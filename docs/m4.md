After conducting these interviews we gathered more insights that confirmed our initial assumptions: small business owners struggle to analyze their sales data and make informed decisions due to lack of time, expertise, and resources. But in the same time there are small busienesses that already use some digital tools to help them with this process and want to improve their data analysis even more who need a simple, yet effective solution.

## ðŸ‘¤ User Persona

### User persona: The busy local business owner

**Name:** Maria Popescu  
**Age:** 42  
**Role:** Owner of a small bakery  
**Tech comfort level:** Low to moderate  
**Business size:** 4 employees  

**Tools she currently uses:**

- Cash register/POS system
- Excel (very basic use)
- Inventory notebook
- Sometimes Facebook/Instagram for promotion

**Goals:**

- Predict how much to bake - without wasting too much of the ingredients
- See simple patterns - slow days, peak hours, seasonal items
- Make decisions faster
- Keep the business profitable

**Pain points:**

- Existing tools feel overwhelming and complex + little tech knowledge
- No time to dig through POS reports or spreadsheets
- Intuition-based decisions often lead to overproduction/underproduction
- No dedicated data analyst

**Barriers & fears:**

- Fear of overcomplicated graphs
- Doesn't want to hire anyone to manage analytics
- Fear of making mistakes with data uploads

**How Analytico fits her:**

- Chat-based - like texting someone who knows your business
- Prediction-focused - tells her what to bake/order tomorrow
- Simple file upload - uses the Excel/CSV files she already has
- Clear visuals - charts only when needed, not dashboards full of complex data

## Use Cases

Based on the user persona that we created and the insights from our interviews, we thought of the following use cases for Analytico:

### Use Case 1: Uploading Excel and Receiving Instant Insights

**Actor:** Maria ( small business owner)  
**Goal:** Transform Excel sales file into clear dashboards

**Steps:**

1. Mihai logs into Analytico
2. Clicks "Upload Excel/CSV"
3. Chooses his sales file
4. System automatically detects columns (date, product, quantity, revenue, etc.)
5. Shows preview
6. Mihai clicks "Generate Report"
7. Within seconds, the dashboard appears with: best-selling products, worst-selling products, peak hours, loss estimation, forecast for tomorrow.

**Success Criteria:**

- User understands insights without explanation
- No technical knowledge required
